Quality: About This Data Set

I think the overall quality of this data set is quite good, with only monir data missing (less than 20 weeks have less than 100 songs), and 46 songs have no performer recorded. But the format of guest appearance is not consistent.

Is the data complete?

After generated a pivot table counting the number of entries for each week, I think this data is relatively complete in terms of Billboard records. However, the popularity chart provided by Billboard may be free of bias. For example, in the very early stage of Billboard Hot 100, they aggregated data from Best Sellers in Stores, Most Played by Jockeys and Most Played in Jukeboxes, and the radio station most played has been replaced by streaming date nowadays; To what extent the weight of each component chart reflects the reality? Are the top 100s really the most popular 100 songs? This may become the source of incompleteness.

Is the data coherent?

Yes, the number of total singers who had been on the Billboard for each year is quite consitent across years.

Is the data correct?

I think most of the data entries are correct. I extracted the set of performers and did a cross check with Billboard top singer list, turns out most of them are matched.
